It depends on the individual. In Queensland, learner drivers must complete a minimum of 100 logged hours before sitting their practical test. Most students benefit from a mix of private practice with family and professional lessons with an instructor. We'll give you an honest assessment after your first lesson on what we think you'll need.
We cover South Brisbane and surrounding suburbs including West End, Woolloongabba, Annerley, Yeronga, Moorooka, Salisbury, Rocklea, Tarragindi, Greenslopes, Holland Park, Mount Gravatt, Sunnybank, Runcorn, Coopers Plains and more. Both. If you're unsure which to choose, we generally recommend manual — passing in a manual qualifies you to drive both manual and automatic vehicles. We can talk through the options when you book.
Bring your current Queensland learner licence — you must have it on you to legally drive on public roads. Wear comfortable, flat shoes. Your logbook is also useful so we can sign off your hours after each session.
Payment is taken at the end of each lesson. We accept cash and card. Lesson packages are paid upfront. We'll confirm all pricing before your first lesson — no surprises.
We ask for at least 24 hours notice if you need to cancel or reschedule. Cancellations with less than 24 hours notice may incur a fee. We understand things come up — just let us know as soon as possible and we'll do our best to work with you.
